Voracious Bookworm

About

Welcome to my piece of the interwebs canvas! I paint it with book reactions, lists, and other literary things. I grew up on Meg Cabot and mysteries, but have since expanded my reading horizons by participating in reading challenges.

My favourite reading challenge is the yearly Book Riot Read Harder Challenge. So far, I've completed the challenges for 2016, 2017, 2018.

I only buy books on trips or when I can't get it from the library. If I order a book, it's through my local independent bookstore, both to support it and since I love the anticipation of waiting for a book to arrive.

I prefer dogs to cats, tea to coffee, hate movie theatres, love improv comedy and basketball, but nothing beats a good book.

Happy reading!
